Uryu Kamihate

Amagami Sister

Uryu Kamihate is a hardworking and determined student who aims to become a doctor. Despite his serious nature, he finds himself living with the Amagami sisters, leading to many unexpected and heartwarming moments.

Basic Info

  • Full Name: Uryu Kamihate
  • Age: 16-18
  • Birthday: January 21st
  • Goal: Become a doctor
  • Nationality: Japanese

Personality

Uryu is disciplined, intelligent, and focused on his future. He can appear strict at times, but he has a kind heart and gradually opens up to those around him.

Relationship

He develops a unique bond with the Amagami sisters, balancing their lively personalities while slowly becoming an important part of their lives.
