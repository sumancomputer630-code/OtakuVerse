💖 Kaguya-sama: Love is War

📖 Basic Info

  • Genre: Romance, Comedy, Psychological
  • Based on: Manga by Aka Akasaka
  • Studio: A-1 Pictures
  • Seasons: 3 + Movie
  • Release: 2019 – 2022

🧠 Story (Simple Explanation)

The story follows Miyuki Shirogane and Kaguya Shinomiya, the top students of an elite school.

Both are in love with each other… but refuse to confess first.

Instead, they play mind games trying to make the other person confess first.

💥 In their world, "the one who confesses first loses!"
