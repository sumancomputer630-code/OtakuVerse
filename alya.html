❄️ Alya Sometimes Hides Her Feelings in Russian

📖 Basic Info

  • Genre: Romance, Comedy, School
  • Based on: Light novel by Sunsunsun
  • Studio: Doga Kobo
  • Release: 2024

🧠 Story (Simple Explanation)

The story follows Alisa “Alya” Mikhailovna Kujou, a beautiful and intelligent girl with Russian roots.

She often teases her lazy classmate Masachika Kuze by whispering sweet or emotional things in Russian.

The twist? She thinks he doesn’t understand Russian…

But he actually understands everything and keeps it a secret!

😂❤️ A cute and funny romance full of hidden feelings and misunderstandings.
