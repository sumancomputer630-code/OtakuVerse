🌸 Horimiya – Full Overview

📖 Basic Info

  • Genre: Romance, Slice of Life, Comedy
  • Based on: Manga by HERO & Daisuke Hagiwara
  • Studio: CloverWorks
  • Episodes: 13 episodes
  • Release: 2021

🧠 Story (Simple Explanation)

The story follows Kyoko Hori, a popular and responsible girl, and Izumi Miyamura, a quiet and gloomy boy at school.

Outside school, Miyamura is completely different — he has tattoos, piercings, and a kind personality.

After discovering each other's true selves, they grow closer and start a sweet and realistic relationship.

❤️ Horimiya is about love, friendship, and accepting people for who they truly are.
