🌟 ReLIFE – Full Overview

📖 Basic Info

  • Genre: Romance, Slice of Life, Drama
  • Based on: Web manga by Yayoiso
  • Studio: TMS Entertainment
  • Episodes:
    • Season 1 → 13 episodes
    • Final arc (OVA) → 4 episodes
  • Release: 2016 (main series), 2018 (final episodes)

🧠 Story (Simple Explanation)

The story follows Arata Kaizaki, a 27-year-old man who:

  • Quit his job
  • Is struggling in life
  • Has no clear future

One day, he meets Ryō Yoake, who offers him a strange opportunity:

  • 👉 Take a pill → look 10 years younger
  • 👉 Go back to high school for 1 year

This experiment is called “ReLIFE.”

Now Arata must:

  • Relive high school life
  • Fix his past mistakes
  • Learn how to live properly again

⏳ But there’s a catch: After 1 year, everything returns to normal… and people may forget him.
