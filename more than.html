💞 More Than a Married Couple, But Not Lovers

📖 Basic Info

  • Genre: Romance, Comedy, Drama
  • Based on: Manga by Yuki Kanamaru
  • Studio: Studio Mother
  • Episodes: 12 episodes
  • Release: 2022

🧠 Story (Simple Explanation)

The story follows Jirou Yakuin and Akari Watanabe, two high school students who are paired together in a special class project.

In this project, students must live together like a married couple and are graded based on their relationship performance.

The twist? They both like different people!

As they act like a couple, their fake relationship slowly starts to feel real.

💔➡️❤️ A story about fake love turning into real feelings.
